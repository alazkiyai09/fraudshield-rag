QUARTERLY FRAUD INVESTIGATION REPORT - Q3 2025
Bank Rakyat Indonesia - Fraud Management Division

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:
During Q3 2025, the fraud detection system flagged 1,247 suspicious transactions
totaling IDR 15.3 billion. Of these, 342 were confirmed fraudulent.

MULE ACCOUNT PATTERNS:
Pattern 1 - Rapid Fund Cycling: Accounts showing >5 transactions within 24 hours
with amounts just below the IDR 500M reporting threshold.

Pattern 2 - Dormant Account Activation: 89 accounts that were inactive for 6+
months suddenly received large deposits followed by immediate withdrawals.

Pattern 3 - Structured Deposits (Smurfing): Multiple deposits of IDR 49M-49.9M
across different branches to avoid single-transaction reporting requirements.

INVESTIGATION FINDINGS:
- 23 mule account networks identified, involving 156 individual accounts
- Average mule account lifespan: 45 days
- Primary channels: online banking (67%), mobile banking (23%), branch (10%)
- Geographic concentration: Greater Jakarta (78%), Surabaya (12%)

RECOMMENDATIONS:
1. Lower monitoring threshold from IDR 50M to IDR 25M
2. Implement velocity checks for new accounts (<30 days old)
3. Cross-reference beneficial ownership data for corporate accounts
4. Deploy real-time graph analysis for network detection
