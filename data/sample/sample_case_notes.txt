Case 2025-Q3-214
Analyst observed likely mule-account ring with hub account AC-77821. Incoming funds from newly opened accounts were distributed to three external destinations within 90 minutes. Transactions were repeatedly set between 9,400 and 9,900 USD.

Control gaps:
- Existing velocity rule triggers only after five transactions; ring performed four transfers per cycle.
- Device fingerprint reused across two beneficiary accounts.

Recommended action:
- Apply enhanced due diligence to accounts opened in the last 14 days with immediate high-value outgoing wires.
- Add typology rule for repeated near-threshold outbound transfers.
